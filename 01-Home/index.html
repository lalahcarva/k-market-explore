ABOUT KENGSTON MARKET

Kensington Market, situated in Downtown Toronto, Ontario, Canada, is a unique and diverse neighborhood. It boasts a rich cultural heritage and is considered one of the most renowned areas in the city. The Market stands as an older district, adding to its charm and character.
